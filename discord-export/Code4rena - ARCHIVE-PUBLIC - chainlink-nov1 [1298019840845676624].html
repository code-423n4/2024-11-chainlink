Rens | Chainlink Labs 11/12/2024 10:40
Even though we increased the max number of nodes from 32 to 256, that does not mean we will suddenly increase the number of signers significantly. We currently require 6 signatures (f+1, f = 5, nodes = 16, nodes = 3f+1), this will not suddenly grow to be >32 (which would not fit in the v section of the signatures). This would be prohibitively expensive as each sig costs ~7.2k gas. Therefore the max range of the vs is not a concern (edited)
